Brgy. Talisay, Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur, Mindanao, Philippines

Hinatuan is the ideal destination for you if you're seeking a place to unwind. Located in Surigao del Sur, Caraga region, Philippines. Given the town's proximity to crystal-clear rivers, cascades, and hidden springs, both residents and tourists like it. You may experience a variety of cuisines, especially seafood, while staying in elegant yet inexpensive hotels. In this town, you can have fun without spending a lot of money. Hinatuan stands as an enchanting gem, celebrated for its captivating Enchanted River—a mesmerizing azure oasis surrounded by cliffs and lush greenery. This natural wonder draws visitors worldwide with its crystal-clear waters, inviting exploration through snorkeling and swimming. Beyond the famed river, Hinatuan unveils pristine beaches, scenic landscapes, and a rich cultural tapestry, offering a serene escape. The town's warm hospitality, coupled with sustainable tourism practices, ensures an immersive experience in its unspoiled beauty. As one of the Philippines' best-kept secrets, Hinatuan entices adventurers seeking tranquility and the allure of untouched nature, promising unforgettable memories.
