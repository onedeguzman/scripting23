About Soma

The protagonist of Shokugeki no Soma, Soma, grew up watching his father prepare meals in their small eatery, Shukujidokoro Yukihira. He improved his cooking skills while attending Totsuki Culinary Academy. His motivation is to outcompete his father, "Yukihira Joichiro," in a culinary fight by becoming one of the best chefs.

Character

a sharp young man who is constantly striving to elevate his culinary skills. He still prepares food in various styles, such as French, Asian, and Indian. From his errors, he gains knowledge. He is self-assured in his abilities and constantly looks to test the boundaries of his cooking by challenging others to compete. Additionally adaptable and creative, Soma can bypass standard operating procedures to prepare meals, enabling him to come up with his own solutions when he wants to gain an advantage. He likes to play around with food and come up with weird recipes. He is an example of a prominent figure who possesses the traits that are common.
He's daring and even reckless. He demonstrates that despite having the greatest disadvantages, he is more than capable of overcoming the toughest obstacles. Soma is not scared to acknowledge that his opponent is superior to him at that very moment and that he is imperfect, but he keeps fighting. characteristics of Joichiro. His courage, which enables him to courageously face whoever opposes him, no matter how much of a genius they are or whatever disadvantages they may throw at him, gives him strength rather than innate cooking talent. Despite the fact that many of his classmates disliked him—primarily because he was arrogant during his admission speech—they were not all unfriendly.

Skills

Japanese Cuisine - A specialty of his in the kitchen. In the restaurant Shukujidokoro Yukihira, he grew up helping his father serve food. He mastered all methods of flavor balancing. Most of the cuisine he prepares includes rice.

Creative and Resourceful - His levels of culinary ability are different. With few ingredients, he can prepare delicious food.

Live Cooking - He is capable of cooking under pressure. He can draw in customers by demonstrating his competence and effectiveness.

Adaptation - He can pick up new strategies from his opponents in every competition and use them to enhance his cooking skills

Soma grew up assisting his dad. He can prepare food quickly because he is familiar with every step in the kitchen. He can make it right away because he is fully aware of the chef's requirements. He has practical experience working in restaurants. In addition, he received different kinds of training from professional chefs.
